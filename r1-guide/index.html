Getting Started

Unboxing Your R1

Your Rabbit R1 package includes the device, USB-C charging cable, and quick start guide.

1

Remove the R1 from its packaging

2

Charge your device using the included USB-C cable

3

Press and hold the power button to turn on your device

Initial Setup

Follow these steps to set up your Rabbit R1 for the first time:

1

Power on your R1 by pressing and holding the power button

2

Follow the on-screen instructions to connect to Wi-Fi

3

Create or sign in to your Rabbit account

4

Complete the device activation process

5

Your R1 is now ready to use!

Basic Controls

Power Button: Press and hold to turn on/off. Quick press to sleep/wake.

Rabbit Button: Press and hold to activate voice assistant.

Volume Controls: Use the buttons on the side to adjust volume.

Scroll Wheel: Navigate through menus and options.

Connecting to Wi-Fi

A stable internet connection is required for your R1 to function properly.

1

Go to Settings > Network

2

Select your Wi-Fi network

3

Enter the password if required

4

Your R1 will automatically connect to this network in the future
